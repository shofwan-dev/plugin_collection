## Creating an Account

1. Visit the website homepage
2. Click **"Register"** in the top navigation
3. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click **"Register"**
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

## Purchasing a License

### Step 1: Choose Your Plan

1. Go to **Pricing** page
2. Review the available plans:
   - **Single Site** ($49/year) - For 1 domain
   - **5 Sites** ($99/year) - For up to 5 domains
   - **Unlimited** ($199/year) - For unlimited domains
3. Click **"Buy Now"** on your preferred plan

### Step 2: Checkout

1. Review your order details
2. Confirm your email address
3. Click **"Proceed to Payment"**
4. You'll be redirected to Stripe checkout
5. Enter your payment details:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVC code
   - Billing address
6. Click **"Pay"**

### Step 3: Confirmation

After successful payment:
- You'll receive an email confirmation
- WhatsApp notification (if configured)
- Your license will be generated automatically
- You'll be redirected to success page

## Viewing Your License

### Access Your Dashboard

1. Login to your account
2. Click **"Dashboard"** in navigation
3. Click **"My Licenses"**

### License Information

You'll see:
- **License Key**: Your unique activation key (e.g., `ABCD-1234-EFGH-5678`)
- **Plan**: Your subscription plan
- **Status**: Active, Suspended, or Expired
- **Domains**: Number of activated domains vs. maximum allowed
- **Expiration Date**: When your license expires
- **Activated Domains**: List of domains using this license

### Copy Your License Key

1. Click the **copy icon** next to your license key
2. Or manually select and copy the key
3. Keep this key safe - you'll need it for activation

## Activating Your License

### In WordPress Plugin

1. **Install the Plugin**
   - Download the plugin from your dashboard
   - Upload to WordPress: `Plugins ‚Üí Add New ‚Üí Upload Plugin`
   - Activate the plugin

2. **Enter License Key**
   - Go to `CF7 WhatsApp ‚Üí Settings`
   - Find the "License" tab
   - Paste your license key
   - Click **"Activate License"**

3. **Verification**
   - You'll see a success message
   - License status will show as "Active"
   - Your domain will appear in the dashboard

### Activation Limits

- **Single Site**: 1 domain
- **5 Sites**: Up to 5 domains
- **Unlimited**: No limit

If you reach your limit, you'll need to:
1. Deactivate an existing domain, OR
2. Upgrade your plan

## Managing Your Orders

### View Orders

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí My Orders**
2. You'll see a list of all your orders with:
   - Order number
   - Plan name
   - Amount
   - Order status
   - Payment status
   - Date

### Order Statuses

- **Pending**: Waiting for payment
- **Completed**: Payment received, license generated
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled

### Payment Statuses

- **Pending**: Awaiting payment
- **Paid**: Payment successful
- **Failed**: Payment failed
- **Expired**: Payment link expired
- **Refunded**: Payment was refunded

### Cancel an Order

You can cancel **unpaid orders only**:

1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Click on the order you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel Order"** button
4. Optionally provide a reason
5. Confirm cancellation

**Note**: You cannot cancel orders that have already been paid.

## Troubleshooting

### License Activation Issues

**Problem**: "License key not found"
- **Solution**: Double-check you copied the entire key correctly
- Ensure there are no extra spaces

**Problem**: "Domain limit reached"
- **Solution**: 
  1. Go to your dashboard
  2. View activated domains
  3. Deactivate an unused domain
  4. Try activating again

**Problem**: "License expired"
- **Solution**: Renew your license by purchasing again

### Payment Issues

**Problem**: Payment failed
- **Solution**:
  1. Check your card details
  2. Ensure sufficient funds
  3. Contact your bank if issue persists
  4. Try a different payment method

**Problem**: Didn't receive license after payment
- **Solution**:
  1. Check your email (including spam folder)
  2. Login to dashboard and check "My Licenses"
  3. If still missing, contact support with order number

### Account Issues

**Problem**: Forgot password
- **Solution**:
  1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
  2. Enter your email
  3. Check email for reset link
  4. Create new password

**Problem**: Email verification not received
- **Solution**:
  1. Check spam folder
  2. Request new verification email
  3. Ensure email address is correct

## Best Practices

### Security

- **Keep your license key private** - Don't share it publicly
- **Use strong passwords** - Minimum 8 characters with mix of letters, numbers, symbols
- **Enable 2FA** - If available in your account settings

### License Management

- **Deactivate before moving sites** - Always deactivate from old domain before activating on new one
- **Track your domains** - Keep a list of where you've activated your license
- **Renew before expiration** - Set a reminder to renew your license

### Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. **Check Documentation** - Review this guide first
2. **Contact Support** - Email: support@cf7whatsapp.com
3. **Include Details**:
   - Your email address
   - Order number (if applicable)
   - License key (if applicable)
   - Description of the issue
   - Screenshots (if helpful)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I transfer my license to a different domain?**  
A: Yes! Deactivate from the old domain, then activate on the new domain.

**Q: What happens when my license expires?**  
A: The plugin will continue to work, but you won't receive updates or support. Renew to continue receiving updates.

**Q: Can I upgrade my plan?**  
A: Contact support to upgrade from Single Site to 5 Sites or Unlimited.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: Please review our refund policy or contact support for refund requests.

**Q: How do I download the plugin?**  
A: After purchase, go to Dashboard ‚Üí My Licenses and click the download button.

**Q: Can I use one license on multiple sites?**  
A: Yes, depending on your plan:
- Single Site: 1 domain
- 5 Sites: Up to 5 domains
- Unlimited: Unlimited domains
